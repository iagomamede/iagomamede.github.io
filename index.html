Iago Mamede

Stochastic thermodynamics and nonequilibrium statistical physics

I study how microscopic systems exchange heat, perform work and produce entropy under the influence of fluctuations.

My research combines stochastic thermodynamics, interacting spin models, Brownian dynamics and large-deviation theory to understand energy conversion, irreversibility and collective behavior far from equilibrium.
